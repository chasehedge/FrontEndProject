Welcome to Richmond

Richmond Virginia is the capital of the Commonwealth of Virginia. Richmond has been independent since the year 1871. Richmond is located along the fall line of the James river.

Fact Details
Population 226k
Year Incorporated 1782
Region Central Virginia
Classification Urban
Average Income 54K

Law, finance, and governments primarily drive the economy in Richmond. The downtown area is home to federal, state, and local government agencies.

Maymont

Maymont is a 100-acre estate and public park in Richmond. It contains the Maymont mansion and is a popular tourist attraction.
