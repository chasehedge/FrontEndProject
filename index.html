The Beauty of Virginia

Virginia is a constituent state, which means it was one of the original 13 colonies. It is bordered by Maryland, Atlantic Ocean, North Carolina, Tennessee, Kentucky, and West Virgina. Virginia has an old nick name of "Old Dominion". It got this nickname from its loyalty to the exiled Charles II of england durring 1653. The state of Virginia was orginally named after Elizabeth I.

Flowering dogwood: state tree of Virginia

The Dogwood was known for being a strong solid tree. It was used in many buildings. It was the material to make the cross on which Jesus was crucified. He cursed the tree to never grow tall enough to be used in another cricifixion as a dogwood cross.

  1. Small, low- branched tree
